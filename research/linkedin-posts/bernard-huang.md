## Post 1

**Date:** June 23, 2026
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/bernardjhuang_ai-is-using-our-work-then-sending-us-none-activity-7474857489268711424-rQ-J

AI is using our work, then sending us none of the traffic.

I'm not anti-AI. I use it every day. But I also run websites, so I'm seeing the damage up close.

Publishers pay people to write guides, reviews, news, and other useful stuff. Real work. Sometimes weeks of it. Then AI bots crawl the site, take the content, and use it to answer people's questions directly. The reader gets the answer without ever clicking the site that made it. That means no visitors. No ad revenue. No money to pay the writers.

On one of my sites, more than 80% of visitors weren't even people. They were bots scraping content. One of my sites went from thousands of readers to almost nothing. Not because the content got worse, but because AI started giving away the answers before people ever reached us.

AI companies are making billions from work they didn't pay for. The people who made that work are getting cut out. So where should we go from here?

Everyone's selling agents as "do the same work with fewer people." It's exactly backwards.

Agents are a multiplier on judgment, not a replacement for it, and most orgs don't have enough judgment to survive being multiplied.

Hand 20 agents to a mediocre team and you don't get leverage. You get 20x the confident slop, shipped faster than anyone can catch it.

## Post 3

**Date:** June 20, 2026
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/bernardjhuang_i-thought-ai-agents-would-buy-me-time-and-activity-7472369892545961984-L6Kg

I thought AI agents would buy me time, and they did the opposite.

I run about 4 main agents now going day and night, writing, cutting videos, even trading a live account. They never stop, so most mornings I wake up to a pile of overnight work to check, fix, or kill before I've had coffee. I work more than I ever have.

The bet is the curve underneath all of it. The agents don't take work off your plate, they raise what's possible on it. Frontier intelligence is the one thing the entire tech world is 120% focused on. The models keep getting smarter and the tokens keep getting cheaper.

So the math is simple. When your whole product, your GTM motion, your entire stack runs on agentic rails, you inherit every one of those gains for free. The stack gets better while you sleep.

Get on the rails while getting on them is still a choice.
