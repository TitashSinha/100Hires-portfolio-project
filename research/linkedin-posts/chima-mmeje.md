## Post 1

**Date:** June 20, 2026
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/chima-mmeje_something-ive-noticed-with-the-industry-activity-7472624688419028994-iwzR

Something I've noticed with the industry is that the more difficult it is to measure SEO impact, the more we obsess over tracking AI visibility.

When I hear how much some companies are spending on enterprise AI trackers that do nothing else but track barebones AI traffic that is less than 2-5% of your website visibility, I nearly pass out in shock!

Yes, you should absolutely be tracking AI visibility alongside SEO visibility, but you also need to understand and make your peace with this truth.

Your ability to influence and drive discovery from external channels will continue to dwindle. It is diminishing returns, and we're watching it happen IRL.

The best way forward is to build your own content ecosystem that drives direct traffic to your channel, serves as a lead-gen pipeline, and helps you stay top of mind with your user. Then you layer on AI visibility with tools like Moz for 360 coverage.

The only brand doing this on the level I imagine right now is HubSpot. Nobody else, and I find that quite shocking.

Invest in your own channels now. Search in 5 years will yield a lower ROI than what we're seeing today.
